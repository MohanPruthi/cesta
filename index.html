ABOUT US

The objective of the CESTA is to provide a strong platform to the students where they can showcase their technical leadership and communication skills. It also helps them to promote their technical skills which are related to latest Technology. Yet another objective is to build competitive environment amongst the students by creating innovative ideas. CESTA also guides to build practical approach amongst the students. Thus CESTA is a must to join

EVENTS

We conducts a series of technical and non-technical events throughout the year consisting of all possible domains along with the techloop sessions which are organised on a weekly basis. We conduct these events for others to grow and for us to gain the opportunity to grow along as well.

Web-a-thon

So, the wait is over and here are all details you need to take part in :- CESTA presents Web-a-thon
Date of event:- From 27th October, 2022 Timing:- 09:30 Am Onwards

  Rules and Regulations:-
  1. You can register only in a team of 1 to 3 members from our college
  2. The competition will be conducted online in a single round.

UPCOMING EVENTS

IOT

On 6th and 12th december 2022, DTC will conduct a workshop on the future topic IoT. This workshop will be in collaboration with eYantra. The main headlights of the workshop were different types of Arduino board such as Arduino Uno, Arduino Uno R3 SMD etc. and interfacing of sensors and uploading data on cloud. Four examples have been taken as a hands-on such as:

  1. LED glow,
  2.Switch sensing,
  3.Temperature display,
  4. small motor

The participants were also awarded with certificates and kits to continue their journey with IoT.
